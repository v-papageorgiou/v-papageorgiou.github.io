About

Hi! I am a first year PhD student at the Computer Sciences Department of UW-Madison, where I am fortunate enough to be working with Prof. Dimitris Papailiopoulos.

Prior to that, I earned my BSc and integrated MEng from the ECE department of the Technical University of Crete, where I had the pleasure of working with Prof. Aggelos Bletsas on asynchronous inference algorithms for ambiently powered wireless sensor networks.
